I AM MARIA

I have 5 years of competitive experience in athletics. My latest passion is martial arts and currently I'm a blue belt holder in kickboxing.

Leading an active lifestyle has alwayas been second nature to me and I love inspiring and motivating others to achieve their personal goals and feel happy!

For me, a healthy lifestyle is much more than just lifting weights. It’s about stretching, warming up, eating healthily, and getting necessary rest and enough sleep.

I love helping others see and experience what their bodies are capable of with just enough hard work. I make it a goal to help you achieve a happy and well-balanced lifestyle that will minimize the risk of future health-related issues.
